Colour & Material Guide

Materials & Colours

Your material and colour choices are the starting point for a beautiful interior. We break our range into Primary and Secondary materials — here's everything we offer.

Primary Materials (Leather, Vinyl, Leathercloth) are used across panels and seats and determine the dominant interior colour. Secondary Materials (Carpet, Moquette, Furflex etc.) are used across flooring and other areas, with a colour range designed to complement your Primary Material choice.

Primary Materials

Seats & Panels

These materials cover the seats, door cards, and main panels — they define the look and feel of your interior. Choose your primary material and colour first.

A period-correct alternative to vinyl — leathercloth has a softer, more supple feel and was widely used in British cars of the 1950s and 60s. Ideal for correct restoration work.
