Materials & Colours

Your colour and material choices are the starting point for a beautiful interior.

We break our materials into Primary and Secondary. Primary materials — leather and vinyl — determine the dominant colour of your interior. Secondary materials — carpets, moquette, piping — complement your primary choice. Our range is the most comprehensive in the industry.

Primary Material

Leather

Connolly Vaumol™

Connolly is a recognised and admired name amongst today's classic car restorers. Established in 1878, Connolly Leather supplied the leather to Rolls Royce, Bentley, Aston Martin and Jaguar from the earliest days of motoring through to the 1990s. Connolly heritage includes the seats of the House of Lords, Concorde, the QE2 and the Ritz hotels.

Connolly Vaumol™ celebrates the natural qualities of each hide — its unique grain, its patina, its aroma. A premium choice for those seeking the most authentic feel of history in their restoration.

Grained, Smooth and Antiqued

Grained Leather is our most popular choice — mechanically embossed with a consistent grain pattern, practical and available in our widest colour range.

Smooth Leather is naturally soft and unpatterned with a high-quality surface finish — ideal where a refined, luxurious feel is required.

Antiqued Leather has a hand-finished appearance giving an aged, lived-in character — perfect for period-correct restorations where brand new leather would seem out of place.

Primary Material

Vinyl & Leathercloth

Our vinyl and leathercloth ranges replicate the original factory materials with accuracy. Available in the same colour range as our leathers, vinyl provides an excellent and durable choice where budget or authenticity considerations favour it. We can colour match closely between our vinyl and leather ranges, allowing a mixed-material interior to have a cohesive appearance.

Leathercloth is a cloth-backed vinyl used in specific applications — particularly for hoods and covers — and is also available in a complementary colour range.

Secondary Material

Carpet

Wool Carpet

Our premium wool carpet replicates the original factory specification most closely. Natural hand-feel, correct drape. Available in our full carpet colour range.

Karvel Carpet

A durable, close-pile carpet with a tighter weave than wool. Karvel wears extremely well and is a popular practical choice for cars in regular use. Colour-matched to our wool range.

Nylon Carpet

A budget-conscious option with good durability. Available in a core colour range to complement primary material choices.

Secondary Material

Piping & Binding

Piping is the welt sewn into seat and carpet edges — a defining detail of the classic British interior. A comprehensive colour range to match or contrast your primary material. Binding finishes carpet edges in a matching palette.

Secondary Materials

Specialist Materials

Moquette

Looped or cut pile fabric used on seat facings and panels in specific models. Factory-spec moquette in our heritage colour range.

Hardura

Durable rubber-backed material for footwells, transmission tunnels and other high-wear areas. Essential for an authentic period interior.

Armacord

Ribbed, flexible material used in hoods and tonneau covers. Characteristic texture factory-correct for many hood applications.

Furflex

Channel-based draught excluding material for door and hood frames. A critical detail in any concours-quality restoration.

Headlining

Wool cloth headlining in factory-correct colours and weights. One of the most visible interior elements — we get it right.

Underfelt

High-quality underfelt for carpet kits. The difference between carpets that look and feel right, and carpets that don't.

Colour Matching

Can't find your exact colour?

Our colour matching service allows us to supply materials in colours not part of our standard range. We can work from original trim samples, paint references, or photographs to match colours as closely as possible.

Particularly useful for two-tone interiors, non-standard factory options, and restorations where the car's history demands a specific colour combination. Contact us to discuss your colour matching requirement.
