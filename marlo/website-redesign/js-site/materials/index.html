Colour & Material Guide

Materials & Colours

Your material and colour choices are the starting point for a beautiful interior. We break our range into Primary and Secondary materials — here's everything we offer.

Primary Materials (Leather, Vinyl, Leathercloth) are used across panels and seats and determine the dominant interior colour. Secondary Materials (Carpet, Moquette, Furflex etc.) are used across flooring and other areas, with a colour range designed to complement your Primary Material choice.

Primary Materials

Seats & Panels

These materials cover the seats, door cards, and main panels — they define the look and feel of your interior. Choose your primary material and colour first.

Complete Material Library

Every material. Every colour. Every option.

We offer the widest range of trim materials available for British classic cars — sourced to original specification and available in colours that no mass-market supplier can match. Here's a guide to everything we offer.

Leather Grades

🟤

Grained Leather

A consistent, mechanically-grained surface. Durable, hard-wearing, and the most practical choice for everyday restorations. Looks authentic and ages gracefully. Available in a wide range of colours.

⬜

Smooth Leather

A natural, un-grained hide surface with a more supple and luxurious feel. Shows natural hide character and marks more readily — but ages with genuine character. Ideal for high-end restorations.

🟫

Antiqued Leather

A distressed, vintage-looking finish with tonal variation and character built in. Perfect for restorations that want a 'lived-in' period feel from day one. A distinctive and popular choice.

✦

Connolly Vaumol™

The original automotive leather supplier to Rolls Royce, Bentley, Jaguar and Aston Martin. A premium choice that celebrates natural hide character — patina, aroma and handfeel. Available in a curated range of authentic colours.

Vinyl Options

Standard Vinyl

Matched to original factory specification for each marque and model. Available in all original and extended colours — the authentic choice for correct period restorations. Can be welded with original weld patterns for TR and Healey models.

LeatherFaced

Our most popular upgrade — genuine leather panels with vinyl seat surrounds. Matched in colour to ensure seamless visual continuity. Available with our perforated embossed finish to replicate the character of original vinyl weld patterns.

Leathercloth

Used for carpet binding, boot/trunk lining and other secondary trim areas. Available in a matching range of colours to complement seat and panel materials. An affordable and practical material for non-primary areas.

Carpet Types

Wool Carpet

Our finest carpet option. A luxurious deep pile, rubber-backed for flexibility in fitting. Won't spread when shaped over bodywork curves. Available in approx. 20 colours including Black, Red, Blue, Beige, Fawn, Grey, Green and more.

Black Red Blue Biscuit Fawn Grey Green +13 more

Karvel Carpet

A ribbed-effect carpet with hessian backing. The original carpet material for Austin Healey and MGA models. Robust and distinctive. We even know which direction the ribs run for each specific model. Available in 4 colours.

Black Red Blue Grey

Nylon Carpet

100% polypropylene fibres with rubber backing. Flexible, durable and hard-wearing — ideal for restorations working to a budget. Deep pile with a good-quality feel. Available in a range of colours for boot/trunk and cockpit areas.

Piping Colours

Piping is the finishing detail that defines a kit — whether matching your primary material or creating a deliberate contrast. We offer piping in a wide range of colours across both leather and vinyl. Piping colour is your opportunity to connect your interior to the bodywork colour, or to add a subtle accent that ties the whole scheme together.

Other Materials

Hardura

A rubberised floor covering used in boot, trunk and cockpit floor areas of many Jaguar models. Available in the original colour specifications.

Moquette

A woven fabric used in seat pleating and panel work across many models. Provides a period-correct fabric finish that complements leather and vinyl areas beautifully.

Armacord

A ribbed flooring material unique to Austin Healey interiors. Paired with Karvel carpet, it creates the authentic floor finish that defines the Healey cockpit.

Hood & Topping Materials

Canvas, mohair and PVC hood materials in correct period specifications — for open-top models where hood colour is an important part of the overall colour scheme.
