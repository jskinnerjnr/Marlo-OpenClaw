Our Home

The Cheltenham workshop

Based in Cheltenham, Gloucestershire, our workshop is where everything happens. From the moment a pattern is laid on the cutting table to the final quality check before dispatch — every step takes place in-house.

We've been making interior trim here since the early days of the business, and the workshop has evolved significantly over the decades — embracing new technology while keeping the traditional craft skills that define what we do.

We don't take cars into the workshop for trimming — but we do offer a re-trimming service for parts that can be removed and delivered to us. Seat frames, metalwork, dashboards — if it comes out of the car, we can trim it.

Our Technology

Modern precision. Traditional craft.

We've embraced modern technology within a traditional industry — enabling us to deliver exceptional interior trim with the accuracy that the original manufacturers would have envied.

🖥️

Digital CNC Cutting

Our on-site CNC machine cuts every piece with millimetre precision. Every notch, drill hole and shaped curve is placed exactly right — eliminating the risk of human error and ensuring consistency across every kit we produce.

⚡

HF (High Frequency) Welding

We use our on-site HF welder to add original specification weld patterns to vinyl cushions, backrests and panel work. We are one of only a few companies in the world to hold the original weld template designs for Austin Healey BJ8 and Triumph TR models.

🧵

Seiko & Singer Sewing

The nostalgic touch: our carpet kits are bound and sewn with Seiko and Singer sewing machine models that would have been seen on the original factory production lines. Traditional equipment doing what it was made to do.

📐

Pattern Archive

A physical archive of original seat and trim patterns, built over 45 years. All patterns now digitised into our CNC library — but we still reference original trim in the workshop regularly for accuracy. You can't build patterns like this from a computer. You need the cars.

⚙️

Leather Embossing

For leather seat upgrades, we offer an embossed 'Perforated' finish that replicates the visual character of the original vinyl weld patterns — giving leather seats the period-correct look while delivering genuine leather luxury.

🔍

Quality Control

Every kit is checked before it leaves our workshop. No conveyor belt, no automated dispatch — every item is reviewed by our team. Small workshop. Personal responsibility. It matters to us.

The Process

How your kit is made

Every kit is made to order. No stock-room pulls. No off-the-shelf guesswork. Here's what happens after you place your order.

1

Your order is received and reviewed

We review your order details — car model, variant, material choices and colour specifications. If anything needs clarifying, Jonathon will get in touch personally to confirm before we start.

2

Patterns are pulled from the digital archive

Your car's specific patterns are loaded into the CNC system. Model variant, series, and any specification details are all accounted for — this is where the 45 years of pattern knowledge pays off.

3

Materials are cut with millimetre precision

The CNC machine cuts your materials — leather, vinyl, carpet, hardura, moquette — with exact placement of every notch, hole and edge. No hand-cutting inconsistency. Every piece identical to spec.

4

Sewing and assembly

Our machinists sew, bind and assemble the kit. Seat covers are pleated and sewn. Carpet is bound and edged. Panels are finished. This is where the craft lives — and where our team's experience shows.

5

Quality check and dispatch

Every kit is checked before it leaves the workshop. Then carefully packed — ready to ship to you, wherever you are in the world. Most orders ship within our stated lead time, and we'll keep you informed throughout.
