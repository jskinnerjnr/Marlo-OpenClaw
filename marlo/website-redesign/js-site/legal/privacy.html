Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Who We Are

John Skinner (Manufacturing) Ltd ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect information you provide to us via our website or when placing an order.

2. What Data We Collect

  • Name, address, email and phone number when you request a quote or place an order
  • Vehicle details you provide to enable us to fulfil your order
  • Communication records (emails, messages) relating to your enquiry or order
  • Basic analytics data (page visits, browser type) via anonymised tools

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to:

  • Process and fulfil your order
  • Communicate with you about your enquiry or order status
  • Meet our legal and accounting obligations
  • Improve our products and website (anonymised data only)

We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data on the basis of contract performance (to fulfil your order), legitimate interests (to communicate with you effectively) and legal obligation (accounting and tax records).

5. Data Retention

We retain order and customer records for 7 years to comply with HMRC requirements. Enquiry data where no order was placed is deleted after 2 years.

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct or delete your personal data, to object to processing, and to data portability. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at sales@john-skinner.co.uk.

7. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies — primarily for analytics. No personally identifiable tracking cookies are used without consent. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data. Our online customer portal uses encrypted connections and secure authentication. No data transmission over the internet is completely secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version will always be available on this page.

10. Contact

For data protection enquiries, contact us at sales@john-skinner.co.uk or write to us at Unit 8, Manchester Park, Tewkesbury Road, Cheltenham, GL51 9EJ.
