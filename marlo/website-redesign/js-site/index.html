Why John Skinner

This is what 45 years of focus looks like

We do one thing. We've done it since 1980. Our focus on a handful of marques means our knowledge goes deeper than any generalist trimmer can match — and it shows in every kit we make.

We're more expensive than some alternatives. Deliberately so. The materials cost more, the patterns are original spec, and workshop skill isn't a commodity. Our customers know the difference — and they don't buy twice.
