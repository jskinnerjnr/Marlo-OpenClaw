About / Our Philosophy

Why John Skinner?

Passionate about quality & service.

From our skilled team in the workshop, to our investment in new production technologies, every part of our business has been developed to deliver the best possible interior trim restoration kits. The pursuit of quality is endless — that's why we'll never stop looking to improve. When a business develops out of a hobby, you know that dedication is at its heart.

One industry. One standard. Since 1980.

We do one thing. We've done it since 1980. Our focus on Jaguar, Triumph TR, Austin Healey and MGA means our knowledge goes deeper than any generalist trimmer can match. We are not a general upholstery shop that happens to do classic cars. We are a specialist that has spent four decades obsessing over the originals.

John Skinner didn't start this company to get rich. He started it because he loved these cars and couldn't find anyone who made the trim to the standard they deserved. That passion has never left the building.

Original specification — not approximate

Every pattern we use has been developed from original factory samples. Our archive of original trim gives our team a direct reference for how these interiors were built. Getting it right requires this level of commitment. Cheaper alternatives simply have not made it.

Made in Britain. Always.

We cut, sew and trim everything in our Cheltenham workshop. We don't outsource. We don't import pre-made kits. When an order leaves our workshop, every part of it has been handled by our team. Each staff member sees a whole job through from start to finish.

The widest material range in the industry

Leather in three finishes including Connolly Vaumol™. Vinyl colour-matched to our leathers. Wool, Karvel and Nylon carpet. Moquette, Hardura, Armacord, Furflex. A piping range to match or contrast with any primary material. We hold the stock. You have the creative freedom.

Expertise you can talk to

When you call us, you get someone who genuinely knows these cars — who can advise on original colour specifications, guide your trimmer on fitting, and work through any issue that arises. That relationship is part of what you buy when you buy from John Skinner.
