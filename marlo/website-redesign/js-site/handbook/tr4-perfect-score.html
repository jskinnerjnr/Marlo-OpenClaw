Notable Restoration

TR4 Wins Perfect 100 Point Score

The first ever perfect score awarded by the Triumph Register of America — and our trim was at the heart of it.

← Back to Handbook
100

Perfect Score · Triumph Register of America · 2018

John Skinner's proudly supplied Mark Macy of Macy's Garage, Ohio, USA, with a number of interior trim parts for his 1961 Triumph TR4 Surrey Top restoration.

The car went on to scoop Best of Show at the national meet of the Triumph Register of America (TRA) in 2018, achieving the first ever 100 Point Score awarded by the TRA.

A Testament to Accuracy

Concours judging of this standard demands absolute accuracy to original specification — every material, every seam, every colour detail is scrutinised. The fact that our trim contributed to a perfect score is a testament to our pattern archive, our material knowledge, and our team's commitment to getting every detail right.

This isn't an accident. It's the result of 45 years of focused expertise in exactly these cars — a dedication that no generalist trimmer can replicate.

What We Supplied

We manufactured and supplied interior trim parts to original specification for the 1961 Triumph TR4 Surrey Top restoration — all produced in our Cheltenham workshop using original specification patterns developed over decades of working with the full Triumph TR range.
