Bespoke Service

Our Bespoke Service

We understand that an 'as original' restoration may not give you the interior you dreamed of. With our bespoke service, we work with you to create something truly yours.

← Back to Handbook

With our bespoke service, we work with you to create the interior you have always wanted by combining highest quality materials with our in-depth knowledge of trim options and finishes. We can elevate your restoration trim kit by upgrading materials from original specification.

Our comprehensive range of materials also means that you don't need to feel limited by colour. Unlike other retailers, we offer multiple shade options across each of our material categories — which gives you a level of creativity and choice not possible with mass-manufactured off-the-shelf kits.

Full Leather Restoration Kits

We can use Leather across all parts originally supplied in Vinyl or Leathercloth to bring a true sense of luxury to your interior — from Door Panels to Hood Frame Envelope covers. Our comprehensive material library covers three finishes of leather: Grained, Smooth and Antiqued — each finish bringing its own appearance and character to an interior.

Connolly Vaumol™ Leather

We also offer Connolly Vaumol™ as an option across our trim kits. As the original leather supplier to many prestigious British classic car marques, the Connolly tannery and manufacturing processes have a steeped heritage within the automotive industry. By celebrating the unique natural qualities of each hide through graining, patina, aroma and handfeel, Connolly Vaumol™ is a good choice for those keen to retain a feel of authenticity and history across their restoration.

Leather Welding and Embossing

For those wanting to upgrade from vinyl to leather across seats and panel work, we can still offer original weld lines across our panels and embossed patterns across our seats. Originally only seen on vinyl, we have created a method that allows us to add HF weld lines across our leather panels — allowing customers to upgrade their material choices without compromising on original details.

Bespoke Colour Options

Beyond our standard colour library, we are happy to work with you on bespoke colour requirements for that truly one-of-a-kind interior. Whether you want to match your bodywork exactly, or take your interior in a completely new direction, get in touch and we'll explore what's possible.
