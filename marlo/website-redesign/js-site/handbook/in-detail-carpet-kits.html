In Detail: Our Carpet Kits

Renowned worldwide for their excellent fit and finish — crafted from highest quality materials, cut with precision on our CNC machine and assembled by our skilled machinists.

← Back to Handbook

Over the years, we have adapted our carpet kits to allow for greater flexibility in fitting. We have updated production-line patterns without compromising originality and our kits are designed to accommodate modern-day soundproofing technology should your restoration demand.

The nostalgic part? Our carpet kits are bound and sewn with the Seiko and Singer sewing machine models that would have been seen on the original factory production lines.

Expert Knowledge of Original Materials

When it comes to car flooring, we really know our stuff. We have developed all our Carpet Kits with a highly informed level of knowledge which not only informs good fit, but also accurate material allocation throughout the car cockpit.

Whilst some models simply have carpet running through the whole flooring area (such as the Triumph TRs), other models (such as the Jaguar E-Types) feature carpet, hardura, moquette and vinyl materials in a variety of combinations depending on the year of manufacture. Austin Healeys were unique in their use of a ribbed flooring material called Armacord, complemented by a ribbed effect carpet known as Karvel.

Wool

A truly beautiful material, our Wool carpet is of exceptional quality; luxurious to the touch with a deep, springy pile and befitting of high-end restorations. The rubber backing provides flexibility and malleability in fitting whilst also holding the carpet firmly in place once in the car. Available in a range of around 20 colours.

Karvel

Karvel carpet has a ribbed effect to the surface and is finished with a hessian backing. Its ribbed weave makes it a sturdy and robust material, suitable for day-to-day automotive use. Most commonly seen throughout Austin Healey and MGA interiors. Available in 4 colours: Black, Blue, Red and Grey.

Nylon

Our tufted nylon is a man-made material that offers an affordable option for those not wanting to go to the expense of wool. With a deep pile of 100% polypropylene fibres and a rubber backing, it is flexible, durable and hard wearing. Ideal for restorations working to a budget.

Precision CNC Cutting

In recent years, we have invested heavily in technology that cuts our materials with a level of precision that could never be achieved by hand. Our digital CNC machine ensures every piece is cut with millimetre accuracy — notches, drill holes, and all shaped pieces are placed perfectly, every time.

The result is a carpet kit that drops into the car correctly, without the need for excessive trimming or adjustment. Our meticulous pattern archive — built over 45 years of working exclusively with these marques — means every cut is informed by genuine, original specification knowledge.
