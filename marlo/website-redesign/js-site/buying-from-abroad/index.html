From Quote to Your Door

How It Works

Request a Quote

Tell us your car, year, trim colour preferences and any specific requirements. We'll come back with a detailed, itemised quote.

Confirm & Pay

Accept the quote and pay by card, bank transfer or Wise. We accept all major currencies. Your order goes straight into production.

Production

Most full kits take 8–12 weeks. We can arrange part-shipments on longer orders so you can start your restoration while we finish the remaining items.

Shipment

Dispatched via FedEx, DHL, UPS or DPD — tracked, signed and insured. We'll email you all shipping documentation before it leaves our factory.

Delivery

Delivered to your door. Any customs requirements handled cleanly with our full documentation pack. No surprises.

Why International Customers Choose Us

What You Get That Nobody Else Can Offer

Genuinely Made in Britain

Every kit is cut, sewn and finished by hand in our Cheltenham workshop. Not outsourced. Not imported components re-labelled. Made here, by people who know these cars.

Often Cheaper Than You'd Expect

Even including shipping and local import tax, a John Skinner kit frequently comes in at or below the cost of locally sourced alternatives — and with a quality no domestic supplier can match.

Fully Insured, Fully Tracked

Every shipment is insured to full replacement value and tracked from our workshop to your door. Via FedEx, DHL, UPS or DPD — whichever is most reliable for your destination.

Card, UK bank transfer or Wise — whichever suits you. Wise lets you pay in your own currency at the real exchange rate, with no hidden fees.
