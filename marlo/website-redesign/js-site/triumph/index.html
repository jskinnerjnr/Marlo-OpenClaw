About our Triumph TR kits

The Triumph TR range spans 23 years and several generations — each with its own trim specifications and quirks. We know them all.

We've been making TR trim kits since 1980. The patterns are original, the materials are correct, and we know where the spec changed between models and production years.

If you're not certain which trim specification applies to your car, tell us the chassis number. We'll identify exactly what you need before you commit to anything.
