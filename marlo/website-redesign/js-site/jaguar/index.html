About Our Jaguar Kits

We've been manufacturing Jaguar interior trim since 1980. In that time, we've developed a depth of knowledge about these cars that very few people in the world can match.

Every kit is made to original factory specification — not an approximation, not a generic fit. We hold original patterns for all the models listed above, refined over decades of production and customer feedback.

Our materials are sourced from the same quality suppliers we've worked with for years. We don't substitute for cheaper alternatives. The result is a kit that fits correctly, looks correct, and lasts.

Not sure which specification is right for your car? That's what we're here for. Tell us your chassis number and we'll identify exactly what you need.
