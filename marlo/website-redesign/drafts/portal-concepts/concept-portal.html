Shipping cost calculated after items are confirmed
Shipping is charged by weight — either the actual weight of the package or its volumetric weight (calculated from dimensions), whichever is greater. Until you've confirmed which items you need, we can't give you an accurate figure. We'll add it to this quote once you're ready for review.
📦
Carefully packed in Cheltenham
Every order is hand-packed by us. Full kits are heavy — leather seat covers for an E-Type run to around 8–12 kg. We use double-walled boxes, appropriate void fill and custom wrapping for leather items to avoid creasing in transit.
🚚
Dispatched with a major carrier
We use FedEx, DHL, UPS or DPD depending on destination and parcel weight. All shipments are fully tracked, require a signature on delivery, and are insured for the full declared value of the goods.
📍
Tracked end to end
You'll receive a tracking number by email as soon as the parcel is collected. Most UK deliveries arrive within 1–2 working days of dispatch. International delivery times vary by destination — typically 3–7 working days for Europe and 5–10 for the rest of the world.
🌍
International orders & customs
We export worldwide. For orders outside the UK, we complete all export documentation. Your parcel will pass through your country's customs — duties and local taxes (VAT/GST/sales tax) are calculated and collected at the border by your customs authority, not by us. Most carriers will contact you in advance with details. EU customers benefit from 0% import duty on UK-manufactured goods under the UK–EU Trade and Cooperation Agreement — you pay only your local VAT.
📋
Partial shipments available
If your restoration is in progress and you want certain items sooner — seats first, carpets to follow, for example — we can split your order into separate shipments. This is simply a practical option for managing your project timeline, not a cost-reduction measure, and each shipment is priced and tracked separately.
