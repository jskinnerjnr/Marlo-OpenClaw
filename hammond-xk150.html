The Story

How It Started

It was Lockdown 1.0, summer 2020. Jonathon Skinner was working from home when his phone rang — Richard Hammond on the line, wanting to talk interior trim.

Neil and Anthony Greenhouse of Tram Inn Garage, Herefordshire were deep into a thorough restoration of the XK150 FHC. After seeing an example of our work, Richard was so impressed by the quality that he needed to see more. Jonathon was happy to help — extensive advice on originality, colours, specification, and material options followed.

And so began one of the most exciting projects in our workshop's history: manufacturing the complete interior trim for Richard Hammond's Jaguar XK150 FHC.

To complement the Dove Grey paintwork, Richard chose a Cherry Red interior — smooth leather and vinyl, Red wool, moquette and hardura, all made to original OEM specification. We also custom-made a bespoke fire extinguisher cover in Cherry Red leather for the footwell.

The finished result was featured on DriveTribe — an incredible moment for everyone in our Cheltenham workshop.
