Care & Maintenance

Soft Tops & Hoods

Whether your car has a classic woven canvas hood or a later mohair top, the same principle applies: neglect turns a functional hood into a leaking, mouldy liability. Regular care is much cheaper than replacement.

Most Triumph TR, Austin-Healey and early MGB hoods were woven canvas. Heavier than mohair and more prone to mould if stored damp — but very durable when properly maintained.

Plastic rear windows

Never fold a cold plastic rear window — it will crack along the fold line. Warm it first with gentle heat. Clean only with plastic-specific products: standard glass cleaners contain ammonia which hazes and yellows the plastic.
