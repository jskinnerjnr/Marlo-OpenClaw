classic British car restoration

Production data, colour schemes, club directories, events and suppliers — all in one place.

Browse Cars → Find a Club

MarqueFile exists because the information you need to restore, buy or maintain a classic British car is scattered across decades of forum posts, out-of-print books and the fading memory of a dwindling group of experts. We’ve pulled it together.

You’ll find production data and chassis number breakdowns, factory colour codes with interior pairing guides, model-by-model buying checklists, and valuation ranges that reflect the real market. Alongside that: a directory of the clubs, events, suppliers, workshops and storage facilities that matter to serious owners.

We cover Triumph TR and Jaguar today — and we’re expanding into Austin-Healey, MG and beyond. Every piece of data is cross-referenced against club records, Heritage Certificates and primary sources. When we’re not certain, we say so.
