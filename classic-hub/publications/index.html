Publications

Club magazines, independent titles and newsletters covering Triumph TR and Jaguar — where to find the best technical content, market intelligence and restoration guides.

Club publications

Included with club membership — these are often the best source of model-specific technical knowledge, written by marque experts for owners.

TR Action
Club magazine Monthly Triumph
Published by TR Register
The official monthly magazine of the TR Register. 60+ pages covering technical articles on all TR models, restoration features, members' cars, competition reports and club news. One of the best single-marque club magazines in the UK.
What to expect
  • Technical deep-dives on TR2–TR8
  • Colour photography throughout
  • Restoration and buying guides
  • Events calendar and reports
  • Members' classified ads
💷 Included with TR Register membership (£48/yr)
Visit tr-register.co.uk →
Courier
Club magazine Bi-monthly Triumph
Published by Triumph Sports Six Club
Bi-monthly magazine from the Triumph Sports Six Club with a strong emphasis on the six-cylinder TR models. Excellent technical content on the Lucas PI injection system and the GT6, Vitesse and Herald alongside TR5 and TR6.
What to expect
  • PI injection technical advice
  • TR5, TR6 and TR250 coverage
  • Concours preparation guides
  • Regional event reports
  • Specialist directory
💷 Included with TSSC membership (£45/yr)
Visit tssc.org.uk →
Jaguar Enthusiast
Club magazine Monthly Jaguar
Published by Jaguar Enthusiasts Club
One of the longest-running Jaguar-specific magazines, published monthly. Covers the full Jaguar range from pre-war to modern, with particular depth on E-Type, XK and Mk II. Market reports and buying guides are consistently useful.
What to expect
  • Monthly — 12 issues per year
  • E-Type, XK, Mk II and XJ coverage
  • Market reports and valuations
  • Factory history and Heritage features
  • Concours and events coverage
💷 Included with JEC membership (£45/yr)
Visit jec.org.uk →
The Jaguar Driver
Club magazine Monthly Jaguar
Published by Jaguar Drivers Club
Monthly magazine from one of the oldest Jaguar clubs (est. 1956). Strong motorsport coverage alongside restoration and technical content. Good on the history and competition record of Jaguar models.
What to expect
  • Strong motorsport and racing coverage
  • Historical features
  • All Jaguar models covered
  • Concours results and judging guides
  • Club news and events
💷 Included with JDC membership (£48/yr)
Visit jaguardriver.co.uk →
Classic & Sports Car
Independent Monthly All marques
Published by Haymarket Media Group
The UK's best-selling classic car magazine. Broad coverage of all marques with authoritative road tests, market guides and auction reports. Strong on Triumph TR and Jaguar E-Type — regularly featured.
What to expect
  • Market values and buying guides
  • Road tests of restored classics
  • Auction results and analysis
  • Wide marque coverage
  • Available in print and digital
💷 ~£6.50 per issue / ~£65/yr subscription
Visit classicandsportscar.com →
Practical Classics
Independent Monthly All marques
Published by Bauer Media
Practical, hands-on classic car magazine focused on restoration and maintenance rather than collecting. Excellent step-by-step guides, buyer's checklists and practical advice — well suited to owners doing their own work.
What to expect
  • Step-by-step restoration guides
  • MOT preparation and advice
  • Buyer's guides for specific models
  • Budget restoration features
  • Strong Triumph and Jaguar content
💷 ~£5.99 per issue / ~£60/yr subscription
Visit practicalclassics.co.uk →
Classic Cars
Independent Monthly All marques
Published by Future Publishing
Long-established mainstream classic car magazine. Good broad coverage with market reports, road tests and restoration features. Tends toward the higher end of the market but covers Triumph TR and Jaguar regularly.
What to expect
  • Monthly market reports
  • Road tests and comparisons
  • Auction previews and results
  • Feature cars and collections
  • Buying guides
💷 ~£6.25 per issue / ~£60/yr subscription
Visit classiccarsmagazine.co.uk →
Octane
Independent Monthly All marques
Published by Dennis Publishing
Premium classic and performance car magazine. Beautifully produced with a focus on significant and rare cars. E-Type and early TR content appears regularly. Better suited to collectors than restorers.
What to expect
  • Premium photography and production
  • Rare and significant car features
  • Concours and events coverage
  • Collector market analysis
  • Strong on Jaguar and early TR
💷 ~£7.50 per issue / ~£75/yr subscription
Visit octane-magazine.com →
