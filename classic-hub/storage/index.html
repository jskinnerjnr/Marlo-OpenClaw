Preparing Your Car for Storage

Before you hand over the keys

✓Change the oil before storage — used oil is acidic and corrodes internals
✓Fill the fuel tank and add stabiliser for storage over 3 months
✓Inflate tyres to upper limit — avoid flat spots during storage
✓Use a quality trickle charger (CTEK or Optimate) for long-term use
✓Cover with a breathable cotton dust cover — never airtight plastic
✓Leave windows open 1cm if the roof is sound — prevents musty interior
✓Place rodent deterrents (not poison) — mice destroy wiring and leather
✓Photograph condition thoroughly before handing over to any facility
✓Check your insurance covers off-road storage — many standard policies exclude it
