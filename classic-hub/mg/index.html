MG

MG built some of the most successful and widely-loved British sports cars in history. From the elegant MGA to the long-running MGB — the best-selling British sports car of all time — and the nimble Midget, these are cars that defined accessible performance for a generation.

All MG sports car models
MGA
1955–1962
101,476 produced
MG's first modern sports car. Elegant envelope body by Syd Enever, replacing the pre-war-style TF. Four variants including the rare and unreliable Twin Cam. 5,869 UK sales — the rest all exported.
