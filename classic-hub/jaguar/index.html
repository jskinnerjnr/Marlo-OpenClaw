Jaguar

From the XK120 to the XJ6 Series 3 — production data, colour specifications, chassis records and buying guides for Jaguar's most celebrated classic models.

Classic Jaguar models
XK120
1948–1954
~12,055 produced
The car that saved Jaguar. Launched 1948 as a styling exercise — demand forced production. 132.6mph world record at Jabbeke 1949. The XK twin-cam engine powered Jaguars until 1992.
