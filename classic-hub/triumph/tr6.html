Overview

The TR6 is the best-selling TR of all time and arguably the definitive sports car of its era. Styled by Karmann of Germany, it retained the proven TR5 drivetrain — a 2498cc straight-six with Lucas fuel injection on UK-spec cars producing 150bhp. American emissions regulations meant US-market cars received twin carburettors and a detuned 104bhp. The TR6 ran in essentially unchanged form for seven years, a testament to the correctness of the design.

⚠️ Buyer's note: Chassis numbers alone don't confirm UK vs US specification. Always verify the fuel system — injected cars command a significant premium.

Interior colours & pairings
