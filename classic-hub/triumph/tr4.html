TR4 vs TR4A

The TR4 was a revolution in TR design — styled by Giovanni Michelotti, it brought wind-up windows, a proper cockpit and rack-and-pinion steering to the TR line. The TR4A (1965–1967) added independent rear suspension as an option (IRS cars are identifiable by their wider rear track) and the famous Surrey Top — a removable centre roof panel that anticipates the modern targa.
